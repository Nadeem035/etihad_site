THE ENGINEERS

HSE ENGINEERS

Our safety officers ensure that every project site is compliant, secure, and risk-free through strict monitoring and proactive measures.

  • HSE Compliance:enforcing international safety standards and local regulations.
  • Site Inspections & Audits:daily monitoring and corrective actions.
  • Toolbox Talks & Training:continuous awareness for workers and staff.
  • Emergency Preparedness:evacuation plans, drills, and first aid response.
  • Risk Assessment & Mitigation: identifying hazards and applying preventive measures.

MECHANICAL ENGINEERS

Our experts have designed and executed advanced mechanical systems that integrate multiple disciplines with precision

  • HVAC Systems:(Heating, Ventilation & Air Conditioning).
  • Fire Fighting & Fire Suppression Systems:(Sprinklers, Hose Reels, FM200, Inert Gas Systems, etc.).
  • Plumbing Systems:(Water Supply, Drainage, Sanitary, Storm Water).
  • Chilled Water Networks:(district cooling, piping, pumps, etc.).
  • Infrastructure MEP Works:(utility diversions, underground piping, chambers).
  • Medical / Specialized Gas Systems:(hospitals, labs, if applicable).
  • Irrigation Systems:(for landscaping projects).
  • Compressed Air Systems:(industrial facilities, workshops).
  • Fuel & Gas Piping Networks:(diesel, LPG, natural gas, where required).

ELECTRICAL ENGINEERS

Our specialists deliver complete electrical solutions that power efficiency, safety, and reliability across every project

  • Power Distribution Systems:LV & MV networks, transformers, switchgear.
  • Lighting Systems:indoor, outdoor & faÃ§ade illumination.
  • ELV & Low Current Systems:data, CCTV, access control, BMS.
  • Emergency & Life Safety Systems:fire alarms, central battery, UPS.
  • Infrastructure Electrical Works:cabling, substations, utility connections.

CIVIL ENGINEERS

Our civil engineering team delivers innovative and reliable solutions that form the backbone of every project, from foundations to final finishes

  • Structural Works:concrete, steel, and composite structures.
  • Architectural Finishes:flooring, cladding, ceilings, and partitions.
  • Masonry & Blockwork:walls, plastering, and tiling.
  • Waterproofing & Insulation:roofs, basements, and wet areas.
  • Infrastructure & External Works:roads, pavements, landscaping, and utilities.
  • Fit-Out & Renovation Works:luxury transformations and interior upgrades.

ARCHITECTURAL ENGINEERS

Our architectural engineering experts bring creativity and precision together to shape spaces that are functional, aesthetic, and sustainable

  • Concept & Detailed Design:innovative layouts and functional planning.
  • Interior Fit-Out Works:partitions, ceilings, flooring, and joinery.
  • FaÃ§ade Design & Execution:glass, cladding, and curtain wall systems.
  • Space Planning & Optimization:maximizing efficiency and usability.
  • Sustainable & Green Design:energy-efficient and eco-friendly solutions.
  • Luxury Renovations:high-end finishes and bespoke transformations.

PLANNING ENGINEERS

Our planning engineers ensure every project is delivered on time, within budget, and in line with client expectations by combining technical expertise with advanced tools

  • Project Scheduling:development and control of detailed programs (Primavera P6, MS Project).
  • Progress Monitoring & Reporting:dashboards, KPIs, and performance analysis.
  • Resource & Cost Loading:manpower, equipment, and cost control integration.
  • Risk Analysis & Mitigation:identifying potential delays and corrective actions.
  • Claims & Extension of Time (EOT):preparation of delay analysis and substantiation.
  • Strategic Planning:aligning construction sequences with client milestones.

INTERIOR ENGINEERS

Our interior design team transforms spaces into functional and luxurious environments

  • Concept Development:creative and innovative ideas.
  • Space Planning:efficient and practical layouts.
  • Material & Finish Selection:premium colors and textures.
  • Furniture & Joinery:bespoke, high-quality solutions.
  • Lighting & Ambience:enhancing mood and functionality.

QC ENGINEERS

Our QC engineers safeguard quality at every stage, ensuring compliance with specifications and international standards

  • Material Inspection & Approval:verifying compliance before installation
  • Workmanship Quality Checks:site inspections and corrective actions
  • Testing & Commissioning:validating systems performance and reliability.
  • Documentation & Reporting:ITPs, checklists, and QA/QC records.
  • Standards Compliance:adherence to ISO, ASTM, ASHRAE, and local codes.
